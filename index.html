How It Works

With the Language Learning With Netflix Chrome Extension, you can customize your viewing and learning experience. Here’s what you’ll get:

Dual Subtitles

Watch with two subtitle tracks—one in your native language and the other in your target language. This helps you follow the dialogue and quickly identify unfamiliar terms or structures.

Instant Translation & Dictionary

Hover over any word to see its translation and definition. With just one click, add it to your personal vocabulary list for review.

Smart Playback Controls

  • Slow down dialogue for better comprehension
  • Auto-pause after each subtitle
  • Rewind with ease to review tricky phrases

Personal Vocabulary Builder

Save new words and phrases to study later. Review them with built-in flashcards to reinforce learning over time.

How to Get Started

Step 1: Install the Chrome Extension

Go to the Chrome Web Store and search for “Language Learning With Netflix.” Click Add to Chrome and follow the instructions.

Step 2: Open Netflix and Choose a Show

Pick any movie or series available in your target language. International content works best for immersive learning.

Step 3: Start Learning

Enable dual subtitles, slow down tricky lines, and explore new words with instant translations.
